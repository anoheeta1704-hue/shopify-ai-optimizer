# Contribution Note

## Participant 1 Responsibilities

- Led the product and merchant experience strategy, translating hackathon goals into a polished, business-oriented solution.
- Defined the dashboard information hierarchy, prioritizing insights and action items for merchants.
- Owned frontend design, UI implementation, and styling across the app, ensuring a clean and consistent experience.

- Integrated AI analysis models and orchestrated prompt processing for store review and optimization recommendations.
- Implemented Shopify data handling and secure store connection logic for merchant storefront insights.
- Refined UX interactions around dashboard updates, error handling, and responsive behavior.
- Helped shape feature prioritization by grounding engineering decisions in merchant value and launch readiness.

## Shared Responsibilities

- Collaborated on the overall product vision, aligning features with a marketplace merchant experience.
- Prioritized feature development together to balance impact, feasibility, and delivery speed during the hackathon.
- Reviewed and iterated on the dashboard layout and UX to make the application feel cohesive and startup-ready.
- Partnered on Shopify integration validation and testing to ensure the experience worked end to end.
- Shared ownership of AI analysis implementation, ensuring outputs were practical, actionable, and merchant-centric.
- Worked together on polishing the final submission, including copy, UI details, and product narrative.

## Product Thinking & Engineering Balance

- This project paired strong product thinking with practical engineering execution. One participant focused on product, design, and merchant experience, while the other delivered backend systems, AI integration, and infrastructure stability.
- Both contributors maintained a shared commitment to merchant-first priorities, balancing usability, technical feasibility, and fast iteration.
- The result is a cohesive hackathon submission that blends a refined dashboard experience with robust AI-driven Shopify optimization.
- Product decisions and engineering implementation were closely coordinated throughout the project, ensuring the final prototype is both compelling and technically sound.
